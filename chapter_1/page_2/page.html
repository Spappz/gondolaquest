GONDOLA looks around and reveals the other side of the room for us. Thanks GONDOLA!

On one side of the room, pale blue light leaks in from a thick glass WINDOW. A bulky ARCADE MACHINE sits against an adjacent wall, facing what appears to be a SEALED PANEL on the checkered floor.

Where the walls are otherwise empty, they are adorned with various GONDOLA PAINTINGS, each signed by a certain SEONG.
