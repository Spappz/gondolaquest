GONDOLA and his SMALL BIRD FRIEND find themselves in a dense TEMPERATE FOREST, bracketed on both sides by ROCKY SLOPES.

Not far from GONDOLA's landing site, a cleanly cut STONE STAIRCASE winds into the canopy. Its adjacent trees are wrapped in SLOGANED RIBBONS that scream out in busy colors.

The RIBBONS rustle to greet a warm breeze. It carries the scent of FLOWERS and INDUSTRY.
